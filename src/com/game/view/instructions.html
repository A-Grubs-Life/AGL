Instructions

  1. You must enter a verb and a noun to direct the caterpillar. Ex. eat leaf

  2. Level one goal is to eat leaf and avoid enemies.

  3. Eat leaves and attack foes you can defeat.

  4. At level seven, fight the boss (squirrel) to save your mate, and go to the tree.

  5. For easy game play enter (go godmode)

  6. Get help with "help", "help all", "help defenses", or "help [defense]".
